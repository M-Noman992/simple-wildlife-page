Pakistani Wildlife Wonders

Explore the beauty of nature, one rare creature at a time

Snow Leopard

The Snow Leopard, a symbol of mystery and grace, roams the high mountains of Pakistan's northern regions.

Markhor

Known for its majestic twisted horns, the Markhor is Pakistan's national animal, inhabiting rocky mountains.

Indus River Dolphin

The endangered Indus River Dolphin, one of the few freshwater dolphins, is native to the Indus River.

Black Buck

A graceful antelope, the Black Buck is known for its striking contrast of black and white and its swift agility.

Himalayan Brown Bear

Native to Pakistan's mountainous regions, the Himalayan Brown Bear is a rare and vulnerable species.

Houbara Bustard

The Houbara Bustard, a protected species in Pakistan, is a migrating bird that faces the threat of extinction.
